Form 1040: Individual Tax Return

The 1040 form is like a financial report card that individuals in the U.S. fill out each year. It shows how much money you earned and helps figure out the income tax you owe. You list your income and subtract certain expenses, like money spent on education or saved for retirement. The form then calculates your taxable income and determines your tax amount.

You can also get credits or deductions to lower the final tax owed. If you’ve already paid some taxes during the year, the form helps decide if you’ll get a refund or need to pay more. It’s a way to make sure everyone pays their fair share
