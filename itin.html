How does it work?

An Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) is a unique identifier issued by the IRS to non-US residents who need to file US tax returns or engage in financial activities in the United States. At RAHA Financials, we understand the complexities of obtaining an ITIN and are here to simplify the process for you.
